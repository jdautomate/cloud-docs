Users and Devices Status

Users Widget

This widget is found on the Status page.

Active Users

Shows how many of the provisioned Users are active. For a User to be counted as active at least one of the User’s Devices must be in use.

Active Devices

A User can have more than one Device. This statistic shows how many of the total provisioned Devices are active.

Detailed Status

On clicking the hyperlinked Users widget title, a detailed view of all the User and Device statuses will be shown.

You can expand a specific User’s listing to see the online/offline status of Devices belonging to that User.

Users Filter

Click on the Filters button on the top-right to open the filter fields. Use the fields to filter the listed Users by their connection status or their Device's connection status. You can also filter by the Group that the User belongs to or their Device’s connected Region.
