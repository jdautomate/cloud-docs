Assign specific protocols to a service

Follow these steps to create a protocol-specific Service:

  1. Sign in to the CloudConnexa Administration portal at https://cloud.openvpn.com.

  2. Navigate to Hosts and click the edit icon (pencil) of the Host that you want to edit.

  3. In the Service section, click the (+) to edit.

  4. Select the applicable Protocol(S) from the drop-down menu.

  5. Click Save.

  6. Click the checkmark to save the configuration.
